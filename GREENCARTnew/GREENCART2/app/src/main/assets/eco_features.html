EcoShopper – Buy Smart, Live Green

Explore how each feature helps you shop sustainably with confidence.
1

Eco Brand Directory

Description

A curated, trustworthy directory of eco-friendly brands and local markets. Filter by category (fashion, food, beauty), certifications (Organic, Vegan, Fair Trade), eco-score, and distance to turn intention into informed action.

Key Benefits

  • Cuts through greenwashing with certifications, materials info, and brand eco-scores.
  • Saves time by putting verified brands and stores in one place.
  • Encourages lower-footprint choices by surfacing local and ethical options.

User Interaction

Open Directory → Apply filters (e.g., “Fair Trade, nearby, 4★+ eco-score”) → Tap a brand → View highlights, certifications, and “Visit Store” or “View Products.” Bookmark and follow categories for alerts.

Example

Searching “organic cotton shirts” shows GreenEarth Clothing (eco-score 4.7/5, Organic + Fair Trade). Local pickup reduces shipping emissions and supports ethical labor.

2

Eco Scanner (Smart Product Scan)

Description

Scan any barcode/QR to see an instant eco-profile: eco rating (0–100%), materials, packaging type, recyclability, and verified labels. Get greener alternatives plus a quick “Why this matters.”

Key Benefits

  • Real-time transparency at the shelf — no guesswork.
  • Highlights packaging impacts to reduce plastic waste (packaging is a major driver of plastic use globally).
  • Helps avoid impulse buys that don’t meet your values.

User Interaction

Tap Scan → Point camera at barcode → View eco rating, packaging info, certifications, and “Better Alternatives.” Add to Wishlist or Share to Community; save to Dashboard to track impact.

Example

Scanning a shampoo (rating 62%) flags non-recycled PET packaging and suggests a bar shampoo in paper packaging — switching can avoid ~12 plastic bottles/year.

3

Eco Tips & Alternatives

Description

Bite-sized eco guidance and practical swaps across fashion, food, tech, and home. Includes “Eco Tip of the Day” and curated alternatives to reduce waste, toxins, and emissions.

Key Benefits

  • Turns complex sustainability into simple, do-able steps.
  • Often saves money over time (reusables, refills, durability).
  • Swaps drive measurable impact (e.g., 150+ single-use bottles/year replaced by a reusable).

User Interaction

Open Tips → Choose a category → Explore quick actions and vetted alternatives. Tap a swap (e.g., bamboo toothbrush) → Compare footprint and price → Add to Wishlist or Buy.

Example

“Food” → “Bring your own container.” Learn which stores offer BYO discounts, and find nearby zero-waste shops and suitable containers.

4

Rewards & Cashback System

Description

Earn Green Points for eco-positive actions — scanning products, choosing verified eco alternatives, completing challenges, and sharing reviews. Redeem for discounts, coupons, or donate to environmental projects.

Key Benefits

  • Positive reinforcement that nudges consistent eco habits.
  • Save money via partner rewards and cashback.
  • Convert actions into collective impact (e.g., tree planting).

User Interaction

Open Rewards → See points, badges, and perks. Tap “Ways to Earn” for daily actions. Redeem perks or enable auto-donate (e.g., 10% of points monthly).

Example

After scanning and choosing refill detergent, you redeem 10% off a verified eco home brand or donate a portion to a reforestation partner.

5

Personal Sustainability Dashboard

Description

Your progress hub — tracks scans, swaps, plastic avoided, estimated CO₂e saved, eco-spend, and streaks. Visuals and milestones make your impact tangible using standard conversion factors.

Key Benefits

  • Makes wins visible with trends and milestones.
  • Encourages habit formation with goals and streaks.
  • Helps budget for sustainable purchases (eco-spend vs. savings).

User Interaction

Open Dashboard → Review weekly/monthly charts and badges. Tap a metric (e.g., Plastic Avoided) for methodology. Set goals and reminders (e.g., “Scan before you buy”).

Example

Switching to refills and a reusable bottle shows “~2.5 kg plastic avoided in 3 months” and unlocks a “Refill Hero” badge — motivation you can feel.

6

Community & Challenges

Description

A supportive space to share eco wins, product reviews, and tips. Join monthly challenges (Plastic‑Free Week, Bike‑to‑Work), climb the leaderboard, and earn badges — because change sticks better together.

Key Benefits

  • Social accountability and encouragement improve follow‑through.
  • Collective intelligence: real reviews and hacks save time and waste.
  • Friendly competition boosts engagement without pressure.

User Interaction

Open Community → Browse posts and challenges → Join → Log activities (e.g., tote, package‑free produce) → Earn badges and move up ranks. Follow creators and brands.

Example

Join “Zero‑Waste Lunch Week.” Log your lunchbox, refuse cutlery, and composting. See others’ setups and find nearby bulk stores — easier and more fun together.

7

Learn Section

Description

Curated explainers and DIY guides that build eco-shopping confidence — read labels, spot greenwashing, and understand materials, lifecycle impacts, and circular economy basics.

Key Benefits

  • Informed decisions: decode “biodegradable,” “compostable,” and “recyclable.”
  • Confidence against greenwashing with a simple “proof checklist.”
  • Practical DIY tips extend product life and cut waste.

User Interaction

Open Learn → Browse by topic → Read quick summaries and “What to look for when buying” checklists. Save, share, or jump to Directory/Scanner.

Example

Reading “How to Spot Greenwashing: 7 Red Flags” helps you avoid vague claims and choose products with transparent certification and materials.

Note: Impact estimates are indicative and depend on product use and local recycling/refill options.
